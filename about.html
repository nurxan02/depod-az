Who We Are

Depod, established in 2013, is a leading manufacturer of professional gun cleaning accessories located in Ningbo, China.

We are dedicated to the development and production of high-quality gun cleaning kits and maintenance tools. Over the years, we have made significant breakthroughs in product design, intellectual property, and quality control.

Our professional technical team is equipped to handle OEM/ODM orders, ensuring that we meet the specific needs of our customers worldwide. Our product range includes gun cleaning kits, brushes, maintenance tools, and hunting and shooting auxiliary products.
