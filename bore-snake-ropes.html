About Bore Snake Ropes

Bore snakes are innovative cleaning tools that provide a fast, efficient alternative to traditional cleaning rods. Simply drop the weighted cord down your barrel, pull it through, and you're done!

Why Choose Bore Snake?

  • Cleans bore in seconds - much faster than traditional methods
  • Compact and portable - fits in your pocket or range bag
  • Safe for all firearms - won't damage barrel
  • No assembly required - ready to use
  • Washable and reusable - long-lasting design
  • Integrated bronze brush - scrubs as you pull

How It Works

1

Drop

Drop weighted cord down barrel from breech end

2

Pull

Pull the bore snake through the barrel completely

3

Done

Your barrel is clean! Repeat if needed
